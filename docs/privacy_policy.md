# Privacy Policy — Expiry Check

**Last updated:** 23 July 2026

Expiry Check (“the App”) is an inventory / expiry-tracking tool for shop staff.

## Data we store

- Product details you enter or scan (name, brand, barcode, batch, dates, quantity, notes)
- Staff usernames and passwords you create in the App (stored for sign-in)
- Store / branch names
- Optional cloud sync copies of the above in your own Supabase project

## How data is used

- To show stock and expiry status on the device
- To send local expiry reminder notifications you configure
- To sync inventory and staff logins across phones when cloud sync is enabled

## Camera and microphone

- The camera is used only when you scan a product label or barcode
- The microphone is used only when you choose voice input
- Photos and audio are processed to fill product fields; they are not sold

## Cloud sync

If cloud sync is turned on, inventory and staff accounts are sent to the Supabase project configured for the App so multiple phones can share the same shop data. Access is controlled by your shop configuration and Row Level Security policies.

## Sharing

We do not sell your data. Data stays on your devices and, when sync is enabled, in your configured Supabase project.

## Contact

For privacy questions about this App, contact the shop administrator who installed it, or the publisher listed on the Google Play store listing.
